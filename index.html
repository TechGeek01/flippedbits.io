About Us

This all started years ago as a side project, where I'd offer my skills to friends and family when issues would arise. The more work I did in building computers for others, or troubleshooting issues, the more I was told that I should offer to do this for a living.

I don't currently do any broad advertising, and my services are mostly word of mouth currently, but in doing so, it's generated enough interest for me to make this go somewhere.

I've had over a decade of hardware experience with building, troubleshooting, and fixing computers, and have been doing the same thing in software for even longer. At request, I offer many services such as hardware assembly, setup, and troubleshooting of desktop and laptop computers.
